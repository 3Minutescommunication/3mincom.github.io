About Us

The basic problem majority of parents struggle with is the lack of motivation and interest in kids towards studying. We have tried to solve this problem by creating a gamified reward system-based learning application that links students' efforts to emoluments.

Motivation to Learn

TalentBuddyTM motivates millions of students to learn through reward system. By providing economic incentives to parents, TalentBuddy also prevents school drop-out.

'Adhaar' of student weak areas

TalentBuddyTM creates systemic learning data layer in society, almost like 'Adhaar', using which education providers can access weak areas of 250mn students, in order to personalize their education.

Promise to every student in the country

TalentBuddyTM is not for best performers alone. Each student can win rewards by improving from their existing level, thereby bringing inclusion for every child in the country. This is radical.
